Private by design

PDF operations run in your browser with PDF-lib and PDF.js. NiftyTools does not upload your files. Keep the tab open until processing finishes, especially for large scans or long documents.

Encrypted or password-protected PDFs may not open. Optimization is a safe browser-side re-save, not heavy scan compression, so very image-heavy files may stay close to the original size.
