What this is

Food Shortage Watch combines official recall signals with commodity pressure context and pantry impact heuristics. It is a planning dashboard, not an alerting authority.

Live mode: openFDA recall data may be limited, delayed or blocked. Commodity price data is shown as a local signal pack until a reliable browser-safe feed is added.

Calm prep: watch categories, diversify staples, rotate pantry stock and follow official recall pages before discarding food.
