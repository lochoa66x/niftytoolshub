World radar covers broad public signals. Local radar follows the selected coordinates. This is a context tool, not emergency advice.

Radar settings

Worldwide score ignores your location. Local score uses coordinates for alerts, nearby quakes, air/smoke, network probes, news attention and travel friction.

World vs local

World radar watches broad public signals: space weather, major earthquakes, internet infrastructure, market stress and global attention spikes.

Local radar focuses on the selected place: nearby quakes, air/smoke, weather alerts, connectivity checks, local news pressure and travel friction.
