Early Warning Radar

A public-signal dashboard for weirdness with two separate layers: worldwide signals that affect everyone, and local signals that change with your selected place.

Radar settings

Worldwide score ignores your location. Local score uses coordinates for alerts, nearby quakes, air/smoke, network probes, news attention and travel friction.

Signal split

World radar: space weather, global quake clustering, major internet status pages, public information-seeking and market stress.

Local radar: official alerts where available, nearby quakes, local air/smoke, weather friction, your-network health probes, local news attention and travel friction.

Premium hooks

Paid layer idea: saved locations, alert thresholds, SMS/email/webhooks, daily local briefing, AI explanations and watchlists for custom sources.
