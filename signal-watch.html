Early Warning Radar

A public-signal dashboard for weirdness: space weather, earthquake clusters, internet outages, news panic, market stress and wildfire/air quality. It watches aggregate data, not private people.

Radar settings

Live sources can occasionally block browser requests. If a card says “source unavailable,” the page is still working; that source just did not answer from this browser.

Signal rules

Scores are anomaly estimates, not predictions. The radar raises the level when multiple independent public systems move at once: solar activity, earthquakes, outages, public information-seeking, market volatility and air/smoke conditions.

0-24 normal 25-44 elevated 45-64 weird 65-79 severe 80+ critical

Premium hooks

Custom watchlists, local alerts, email/SMS, webhooks, saved baselines and per-city signal monitors can become the paid layer. The free radar remains public-source and privacy-preserving.
