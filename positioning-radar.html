What this is

Market Positioning Radar mixes different public positioning signals. CFTC futures data is weekly. ETF short interest is delayed. Crypto long/short ratios can be faster, but exchange-specific. Treat this as crowding context, not a buy/sell system.

Important: long/short data is not one universal measurement. SPY short volume, Nasdaq short interest, CFTC futures positioning and crypto perp account ratios answer related but different questions.
