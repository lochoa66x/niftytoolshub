What this isnotes

Outage Radar prioritizes official provider feeds where they exist. Global watch nodes show coverage areas, while consumer apps and phone carriers are labeled as local checks or manual regional links when public APIs do not exist.

Why this matters: internet outages usually cascade. A Cloudflare, AWS, Google, Meta or carrier event can make unrelated apps look broken. This view helps separate platform trouble from your own connection.

Next premium layer: saved cities/providers, multi-region probes, SMS/email alerts, timeline history, and private status watches for your own sites.
