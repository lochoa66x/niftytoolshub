How this works

The compressor draws your image into a browser canvas at the requested dimensions, then exports a fresh WebP, JPG or PNG. Background remover lite samples the corner color or your picked color, makes nearby pixels transparent, and softens edge pixels using the tolerance controls.

For best compression, try WebP at 70-85% quality. For transparent backgrounds, use PNG or WebP. JPG cannot store transparency, so this tool switches background-removal exports away from JPG automatically.
